The Innkeeper

one to three sentence description of game

The Innkeeper is a game about running an inn in a fantasy world. You will manage resources, interact with guests, and make decisions that affect the outcome of your inn's story. Will you become the most renowned innkeeper in the land or will your establishment fall into disrepair?
