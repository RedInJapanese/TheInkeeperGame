The Inkeeper

Travel a post-apocalyptic world, solve puzzles to discover the truth behind the calamity...

The Inkeeper follows a protagonist named Elio, the last of a race called the 'Inkeepers' that can use ink to create objects. He wakes up in this ruinous world where he grapples with isolation and survivor's guilt while trying to find out the truth of what calamity happened to his race.

Interact with other survivors of the calamity. Learn more about the world, make new friends, do quests for them, and get closer to solving the mystery.

Solve unique puzzles at shrines to progress through the world!

Travel the world, discover new things, but remember to save your progress!
